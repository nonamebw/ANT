可以使用postman对灯控制进行简单测试 本地测试地址：127.0.0.1:8080/msg，各功能的json数据包如下：

1.灯的开关
{"type":"write.req","device_id":"9a-b6-ff-00","key":"0.general_onoff","value":"1"}
说明：device_id 终端Id value=1 开 value=0 关

2.灯立即获取状态
{"type":"write.req","device_id":"9a-b6-ff-00","key":"0.general.onoff","mark":"acquireStatus"}
3.改变上报周期
{"type":"write.req","device_id":"9a-b6-ff-00","key":"0.general.onoff","mark":"{\"changeRate\":{\"hour\":00,\"minute\":21,\"second\":00}}"}
